Privacy Policy for MoCa Apps

Effective Date: 4-aug-2023

MoCa is committed to protecting the privacy of our app users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information obtained from users of our mobile applications. By using our apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data Collection

Our apps are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Users must be at least 13 years old to use our apps.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Our apps may collect certain information from users to ensure smooth functionality and provide a personalized experience. The data collected includes:

Device Information: We may collect information about your device, such as the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

Location Data: For apps that require location-based services, such as our compass app, we may request access to your device's location to offer relevant features.

3. Third-Party Services

Our apps may integrate with third-party services, such as AdMob, for the purpose of serving relevant advertisements. These services may collect and use data in accordance with their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this Privacy Policy or with your consent. The data collected may be shared in an aggregated and anonymized form for analytical purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

The data collected by our apps is primarily stored locally on your device. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. User Rights

You may exercise your rights regarding your personal information by contacting us at [info@moca.capital]. If you wish to delete your data, uninstalling the app from your device will remove most of the data stored locally.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our apps do not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect user data.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be effective upon posting in the app store or our website, as applicable. Your continued use of our apps after any changes signify your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the data practices of our apps, you may contact us at [info@moca.capital].

10. User Consent

By using our apps, you consent to the collection and use of your data as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
